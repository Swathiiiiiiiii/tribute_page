VINESH PHOGAT

An inspirational journey of blood sweat and tears

Vinesh Phogat a trailblazing Indian wrestler, has become a symbol of strength, perseverance, and determination in the world of sports. Born into a family of wrestlers, she has overcome numerous obstacles to rise to the top, representing India on the global stage. Her relentless pursuit of excellence and passion for wrestling have inspired countless young athletes, especially women, to break barriers and follow their dreams
