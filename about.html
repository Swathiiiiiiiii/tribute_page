Her life story

Early Life

Vinesh Phogat was born on August 25, 1994, in Balali, a small village in the Bhiwani district of Haryana, India. She hails from a prominent family of wrestlers, including her cousins Geeta and Babita Phogat, both well-known athletes. From an early age, Vinesh trained rigorously under the guidance of her uncle, Mahavir Singh Phogat, a respected wrestling coach who had already made a name for himself by mentoring other family members in the sport.

Education

Vinesh Phogat pursued her education alongside her wrestling career. While her primary focus was on excelling in sports, she managed to balance both her academic and athletic commitments. Vinesh attended local schools in Haryana, where she completed her basic education. Like many athletes, her rigorous training schedule and competitions meant that formal education often took a backseat to her athletic ambitions.
